How GSTSyncPro Works

From file upload to ITC summary in four easy steps. No data leaves your browser.

1

Upload 4 Files

Drag & drop your GSTR-2B Purchase, Books Purchase, GSTR-2B CN/DN, and Books CN/DN files directly into the extension.

Export Results

FAQ
Frequently Asked Questions
What is the difference between plans? ▼
Silver is for 1 user for 1 year (₹120). Golden is for up to 5 users for 1 year (₹240). Lifetime is for up to 3 users with lifetime access and all future updates (₹5,000). All plans unlock the same features — only user count and validity differ.
How do I activate my license? ▼
After purchase, you will receive your license key via email at the address you provide during checkout. Open the GSTSyncPro extension, go to the Activate License screen, enter your email and license key, and click Verify & Activate.
Is my data safe? Does it upload anywhere? ▼
Your financial data never leaves your device. GSTSyncPro processes all files locally inside your browser. Only license validation communicates with our server — no GST data, GSTIN, or invoice data is ever transmitted.
Why am I not getting the 50% discount? ▼
The 50% discount is exclusively available on UPI, Wallet (Paytm, Mobikwik, etc.), and Net Banking transactions. Credit cards, debit cards, and international payment methods are not eligible for this discount.
What file formats are supported for upload? ▼
GSTSyncPro supports Excel (.xlsx, .xls) and CSV formats for all four file types — GSTR-2B Purchase, Books Purchase, GSTR-2B CN/DN, and Books CN/DN.
Can I get a refund? ▼
Refunds are only considered for proven technical deficiencies or service failures caused by Infitax Solution that cannot be resolved within 7 working days. Refunds are not available for change of mind, user-side compatibility issues, or general dissatisfaction. Annual plan requests must be raised within 7 days of purchase. For the Lifetime Plan, issues can be reported at any time — but Infitax Solution holds the sole right to accept or reject any refund. See our full Refund Policy or email Infitaxsolution@gmail.com.
Does GSTSyncPro work offline? ▼
Yes — reconciliation, matching, and export all work offline. An internet connection is only needed during initial license activation and periodic validation checks.
